Hyredesk Recruit- Robust. Guided. Smart.

Empower your recruitment managers. Connect people to purpose and generate qualified applications, deliver results-oriented recruiting practices with embedded engagement and automation to help secure the right talent on any device, and nurture until you find that ideal fit.

  1. 1

    Multi - channel Candidate sourcing

    Hyredesk helps you run your whole sourcing organization on a single platform. Our Chrome extension lets every employee deliver the best candidates with zero parsing headaches. Source candidates in a single click from Hyredesk's candidate database, and leading portals like Naukri, Shine.

  2. 2

    Manage candidates throughout the hiring process

    Our CRM gives you a visual representation of your candidates across various stages of your hiring process. One look at this screen and you'd know your hiring pipeline in a nutshell. Further, maintain a single record of all conversations- calls, emails, SMSs to and from every candidate to keep you compliant.

  3. 3

    Improve interviewing – from scheduling to feedback

    Schedule interviews between hiring managers & candidates. You can send Google Calendar invitations to make sure that there's no conflict while scheduling an interview. You can also update and manage interview status' and feedbacks using the ATS.

  4. 4

    Contextual collaboration

    Keep your team updated with all updates made for the candidates in your pipeline, like all communication with the candidates, interviews scheduled, interview feedbacks etc.

  5. 5

    Conduct Video Interviews

    Our ATS has built in video interview module. You can customize your questionnaire and invite candidates to appear for the video interviews. You can then score the candidates basis their interview and shortlist candidates from just about everywhere without the hassle of scheduling and re-scheduling in-person interview sessions.

  6. 6

    Hyredesk Smart recruiting

    Free your recruiters from tasks that have everything to do with operations and nothing to do with their recruiting skills. Our system is backed with AI, and can handle initial screening of your candidates using our chatbot. Further, can also help grade candidate video interviews.

  7. 7

    Analyse & Strategize

    Our ATS helps you make decisions backed by real-time data about your business. Improve your hiring strategy by measuring your performance and then, generating reports to understand it. You can create different types of reports in our applicant tracking system -pipeline movement, interview success, candidates by source, and so on.

  8. 8

    Track Placements & Send Invoices

    Complete your recruiting process by generating and e-mailing candidate trackers, and invoices to your clients, keep track of unpaid bills & get paid for the work you do!

  9. 9

    Discover our Rich Candidate Database

    Hyredesk not only helps you steamline your recruitment process, but also has a very rick candidate database you can source from! You don't have to get accounts on other job portals anymore. You can find the best talent on your very own Hyredesk itself.

  10. 10

    Vendor Management

    If you're an organization that outsources candidate sourcing to recruitment agencies, Hyredesk has just the vendor management module for you. You can manage all your vendors using Hyredesk, you can review the candidate pipelines your vendors have created for you, connect and communicate with them, conduct video interviews, analyse they performance of all your vendors and much more.
