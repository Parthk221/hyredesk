Smart Hiring Happens Here

See how Hyredesk improves each step of your hiring process.

Applicant Tracking System

Hyredesk gives you a visual representation of your candidates across various stages of your hiring process. One look at this screen and you'd know your hiring pipeline in a nutshell.

Drag & Drop Pipeline

It’s the most user-friendly candidate management you’ll ever meet. Hyredesk will give your team a beautiful, visual hiring pipeline that shows you in a glance where a candidate is at, at any time. Simply drag and drop your candidate's card to the stage the candidate belongs to in your hiring process.

Detailed Candidate Profile

Deep dive into candidate profiles and get the information you need immediately. Add resumes, interview notes, and other support documents and much more. Candidate profiles are rich, informative, and deeply customizable. Have all the information you need to get that placement.

Visualization Filters

Visualizations help you visually sort the current status of your pipeline so you can gain immediate insight so that you can be as efficient as possible.

Create and Assign Tasks

Creating Tasks ensures nothing gets by your team. Assigns tasks to yourself, to colleagues, or globally, get reminders in the app before tasks are due. Tasks are saved to candidate profiles, so that everybody stays up to date.

Video Interviews & Interview Scheduling

Make a positive impact on your candidates by conducting their video interviews, and let hyredesk aid in making sure that the entire interview process, from the time an interview is scheduled, to onsite interview coordination, to quick follow-ups, is now made easier than it sounds.

Video Interviews

Allow your candidates to express themselves beyond their CV and pre-screen them more efficiently. With video interviews candidates can see the questions beforehand, prepare their answers. Define your questionnaire and the answer time and test candidates' knowledge and motivations to get more complete insights, observe beforehand how they present themselves to assess if they fit your expectations. Getting a holistic view of candidates will allow you to decide better who you should move forward.

Video Interview Evaluation

Video interviews don’t require recruiters’ presence when candidates answer their questions. This makes more time available to review even more candidates. Our video interview software allows recruiters to comment and rate all candidates’ answers for a fairer selection, based on the potential demonstrated.

Improve Team Collaboration

Hyredesk allows you to easily share the video interviews with your hiring managers and exchange your thoughts and evaluation, so you can all reach an agreement on which are the best candidates. Deciding which candidates will advance in the recruitment process based on multiple recruiter’s opinions enables better candidate selection. Once a video interview is submitted, it remains available 24x7 and can be watched on any device when and where each recruiter sees best fit.

Interview Scheduling

Get rid of all the hassle of interview scheduling with Hyredesk interview scheduler. It's a win-win scheduling solution that saves recruiters precious time and allows you to send a google calender invitation for the interview scheduled, improving candidate experience. You will drastically cut down the amount of emails sent, and phone calls made to schedule an interview.

Source the Best Candidates

Build segmented and prioritized pipelines for open and future roles with talent pools for all relationships in one place and always up to date.

Our one - click Chrome extensions

Our Hyredesk Chrome Extensions let everyone on your team grab all interesting, new prospects from leading portals like Naukri and Shine. In one click, their profile is imported to Hyredesk as a new candidate profile, without you having to worry about data parsing.

Session Manager

Hyredesk's session manager extension lets you use your Naukri or Shine logins parallely on multiple systems. No more worrying about sourcing from these portals one user at a time. Just keep recruiting with your team.

Talent rediscovery - Leverage existing data

No more losing on the out-of-date profiles in your ATS. Mine your existing talent goldmine with data enrichment and cross platform search across your ATS, and source former applicants who fit open roles.

Data governance

Ensure data integrity across your recruitment stack, remove duplication and enforce robust processes across your organization.

Experience Smart Screening

Let our AI chatbot handle the initial candidate screening for you. Our chatbot can interact with the candidates you've sourced, and help gather initial information from them so you can take the hiring process further in the right direction.

AI driven Email Interactions - Deep Profiling

Share job opportunities with candidates via emails, and have our bot drive the conversation ahead, sharing job details, and gathering candidate information like job location preferences, total experience, preferred industry etc. So you have all the information you need, and get the positions filled.

Smart Screening

Recruiters agree that the hardest part of recruitment is screening candidates within large applicant pools. Hence, finding top talent fast greatly depends on the ability to automate the recruitment workflow through Artificial Intelligence (AI) to more effectively screen and assess candidates. Our ATS resume screening algorithm screens candidates against job requirements in a more reliable and faster way than manual screening.

Hyredesk Connect

Once you find the best talent for the roles you are trying to fill, Hyredesk will help you connect with those candidates, and manage all conversations in one place, displaying it in one clean, actionable view. No more juggling between different apps and communication channels to interact with your candidates.

Emails

Touch base with your candidates over emails using Hyredesk. You can send an email just by clicking on a button. Not just this, manage the candidate responses in the same space. You can access the entire communication thread, and continue the conversation, without having to go to and fro to your mailbox.

Phone Calls

Initiate phone calls with your candidates using Hyredesk ATS. Simply click on the call button, and you'll have a call initiated from your mobile to the candidate's phone.
The call recording will be uploaded on the portal, you can access it anytime, anywhere for reference. Put the worry of having to remember the course and direction of your interaction with your candidate behind, and let Hyredesk manage that for you.

Text Messages

With Hyredesk, you can send a text message to your candidates with just one click. Touch base with your candidates, send out job alerts, invite candidates to video interviews and more without juggling between your phone and your system, simply manage it all from your system.

WhatsApp

Hyredesk helps you connect with your candidates on WhatsApp instant messenger. Simply click on the button, and share job openings with the candidates you've sourced, from your system itself

Analytics, Reports & Call QA

Get a solid understanding of your current hiring status to plan your future recruiting strategy. Uncover the bottlenecks in your process and make data-driven improvements.

Dashboard Analytics

Measuring your recruiting activities is key to know how your recruitment process is performing and to get insightful data to help continuously assess and improve it. Hence, our user-friendly analytics dashboard is a great recruitment management feature. Measure everything regarding job openings, from application channels to time-to-hire, among other top metrics. Use our custom, interactive and real-time reports to have easy access to your numbers and deliver a first-class recruitment process.

Improve Recruiting Performance

Measure team performance: Analyze team activity and track KPIs by reporting on calls, emails and notes logged. Gain real-time visibility into the current state of your candidates and requisitions with actionable analytics. Dive deeper into your data with our out-of-the-box reports like time-to-fill, bottleneck, and source performance. Compare your performance to your peers with our company benchmark report.

Customize your data

Hyredesk's reports are so flexible that you can create your own custom reports in a format that works best for you and that reflects how your team works. It’s easy to make your team look good. Just dynamically select from a few columns and filters into your report builder to create the exact reports that you and your executives need.

Call QA

Using Hyredesk, you can monitor the calls made by your team members to their candidates, and rate the calls. This will help better your call flow and interaction quality with the candidates since you will be able to address and eliminate the gray areas as you identify them.

Candidate Trackers

Hyredesk - Discover Rich Candidate Database

Hyredesk offers a rich candidate database you can source from. Get recruiting from start to finish with our one stop recruiting destination.

Recruit to Onboard

Don't have to worry anymore about getting accounts with job portals to start recruiting. Source the best candidates from various social media platforms. Simply search using your keywords, and get candidates in your hiring pipeline.

Find the right candidates

With Hyredesk’s search, Boolean logic, advanced keyword search, and prioritization of results based on relevance and recency, you can build a qualified shortlist in no time at all. Customize and configure list views and save your favorite searches for even more time savings.

Add sourced candidates directly to your pipeline

Once you've searched for the profiles, directly add them all to your job position so you have a rich candidate pipeline. Vendor Management - Manage your outsourced sourcing

If you have outsourced your sourcing to recruiting agencies, and managing and keeping a track of how your vendors are contributing to fulfill your hiring requirements keeps you and your team on toes, then Hyredesk has got you covered.

Get your vendors under your Hyredesk umbrella

Create accesses for your vendors on Hyredesk, and bring them under your umbrella. This way, not only will your vendors get to experience Hyredesk's smart recruitment, but you too can get them on your radar.

Meet only with the candidates you shortlist

Have your vendors conduct video interviews for their candidates with Hyredesk, and meet only with the candidates that you shortlist basis the video interviews.

Transparent payout structure and invoicing

You can clearly showcase the payout structure for your job positions on Hyredesk. And no more manual invoicing, have Hyredesk do your vendor invoicing for you basis the payout structure set by you. Ever thought vendor management could be this easy?

Analyse your vendor's performance

Now that your vendors too are on Hyredesk, you can analyse their performance using Hyredesk's analytics and reports. Now, you can evaluate and take decisions based on real time data, just as you do for your in-house team.
